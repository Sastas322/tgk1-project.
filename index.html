About

Who is TGK-1

We are Territorial Generating Company No. 1, part of Russia's largest energy holding

What we do

We produce electricity and heat for residents and businesses of Saint Petersburg, the Republic of Karelia, and the Murmansk region. Our stations operate around the clock, providing uninterrupted power to more than 3 million residents.

Our mission

To ensure reliable, affordable, and environmentally friendly energy supply for the Northwest region. We invest in equipment modernization, develop renewable energy, and create comfortable conditions for our employees.

Why we matter to the region

  • Provide 50% of heat in Saint Petersburg
  • 100% heat supply in Petrozavodsk, Apatity, Kirovsk
  • 40% energy from renewable sources (hydroelectric)
  • Among the top 10 largest generating companies in Russia
